# LLM-as-a-Judge

## What is it?

Using an LLM to evaluate outputs of another LLM.

---

## Why use it?

- scalable
- fast
- consistent

---

## Example

Prompt:
"Rate this answer from 1–5 based on accuracy and clarity"

---

## Risks

- bias
- inconsistency

---

## Best Practice

- combine with human review
- use clear scoring rubrics
